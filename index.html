Coach and Mentor Booking System

Welcome to the Coach and Mentor Platform

This platform empowers coaches and mentors in fields such as mental health, career guidance, and entrepreneurship by allowing them to offer their services globally. Clients can easily book sessions using cryptocurrency, enabling coaches and mentors to create job opportunities for themselves without having to depend on traditional employment. Sessions are managed off-chain for flexibility and convenience.

These are the 4 sessions and it's prices in wei

    Individual One Hour Session = 1 wei
    Group One Hour Session = 3 wei
    Individual One Month(4 weeks) = 5 wei
    Group One Month(4 weeks) = 7 wei

You can pay by connecting your wallet and select the session you want and book
